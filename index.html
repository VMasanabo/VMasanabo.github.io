MH Dental Solutions is dental trading company Which was found and established in 2017. It's based in South Africa in Mpumalanga at a small town called Siyabuswa. We specialize in high quality dental equipment. Since 2017, our company has been focusing on dental product export, including dental equipment and materials, dental cultural products and electrical and so on. We believe that the quality of our products and services is the lifeline of our enterprise. Our company has strict control of product quality and partner selection. All of our products are popular in Europe, America , Middle East, Asia Area and more than different countries. While developing long and lasting cooperation with many major companies in these areas, we are seeking for more new partners from all over the world. We have established a strong relationship and effective cooperation with the main dental manufactures. Proudly we do have the leading capability in terms of supplying dental clinic all over of South Africa.
